A friend gave me this poster from a workshop that I didn’t attend. I put it away into a flat folder and the next day I found the perfect frame for it: these frame-less frames that are pared down into glass and clips. I bought two frames and packed them carefully into a bag to {accompany, shuttle, ???} home on the subway. Mid-transit and mid-car and mid-seat-shuffle there was a mid-train-rattle and a man stumbled and a crack was placed in the bottom-right corner corner of the glass.
Just as well that the poster itself says “… a process of letting go, embracing imperfection and being open to experimental results”.
